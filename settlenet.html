About StellerNet

StellerNet is a next-generation satellite constellation launched by SpaceTech to deliver fast, secure, and low-latency internet across every corner of the planet â€” from urban cities to remote deserts and oceans.

Mission Objectives

  • Establish a low-Earth orbit (LEO) satellite network for global coverage
  • Connect underserved regions with reliable broadband
  • Provide secure, real-time communications for disaster recovery
  • Support IoT, autonomous systems, and AI-based smart networks

Network Design

StellerNet includes over 3,000 interconnected LEO satellites forming a resilient mesh network. Each satellite features advanced beam-forming antennas, autonomous orbital correction, and laser inter-satellite communication.

Launch Timeline

  • Phase 1 (2024): Initial 300 satellites launched and deployed
  • Phase 2 (2025): Expanded coverage with 1,200 satellites
  • Phase 3 (2026-2027): Global coverage with 3,000+ satellites

Global Benefits

  • High-speed internet in remote and rural regions
  • Low-latency cloud gaming, streaming, and remote work
  • Emergency communication during disasters
  • Reduced digital divide across the globe
